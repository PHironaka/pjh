Old Spice Hair Care

Jan 20 2014
  • Old Spice
  • Wieden+Kennedy

When a guy has Old Spice hair, great things happen. No, seriously. The hair actually does it. It literally does the thing. We’re serious, guys. Introducing the new Old Spice Hair Care and Styling Product Lineup.

Another in a series of hilariously outlandish Old Spice Campaigns. W+K partnered with Dev Shop Framestore to produce an interactive website, featuring a hair piece playing the sweet tunes of Huey Lewis and The News. Sadly, I was not part of this piece of the campaign, but I did produce flash banners to support the launch! I also helped launch the Old Spice Tumblr during this campaign, which can be viewed HERE.
