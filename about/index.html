.row .span6 :markdown ### The books Back in 2004, before I had any idea how to code, I created a monster. Started with sgf files containing the problem collections, wrote an [awful Perl script][rotate] rotating the problems to the upper left corner. (Hey, it's just 100 lines of code and actually *does* something. These days I write thousands of lines of code to do nothing much at all.) Then used sgf2dg (back when it was called sgf2tex!) to spit out TeX output. PdfTeX to get pdfs and voila. This would be a good place to thank [Toxygen][toxygen]. Do you know how Metafont fonts look when processed with pdfTeX? Fucking ugly. I spent weeks trying to convert the Metafont go diagram fonts into PostScript format. Then Majo came along and converted them, I still have no idea how he did it. If you ever want to produce go pdf's from TeX, get the [PostScript fonts][fonts].
